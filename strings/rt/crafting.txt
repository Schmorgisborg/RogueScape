<h4>Alchemy:</h4><br>
Cauldron: 2 iron bars, 4 stones, 1 small log.<br>
Mortar & Pestle: 3 & 2 stones, respectively.<br>
The Mortar & Pestle can be used to turn many items into respective dust.<br>
The cauldron must contain at least 20oz of water, no matter the resulting potion.<br>
Many items can be turned into dust, using a mortar and pestle.<br>
Feau dust acts as a boost, giving you more potion per brew.<br> 
Feau Dust- 2 iron dust, 1 gold<br>
Glass- Smelt clod<br>
Bottle- 2 glass<br>
Vial- 1 glass<br>

Health- Viscera (ground sinew) and wheat chaff<br>
Strong Health- Add water rune dust while brewing health<br>
Mana- Bone meal and seed dust<br>
Strong Mana- Add rune essence dust while brewing mana<br>
Antidote- Sweet berries and coal dust<br>
Disease Cure- Silver dust and ash<br>
Fortify Strength- Sinew, iron dust, and fire rune dust<br>
Fortify Speed- Pure moondust, salt, and fire rune dust<br>
Fortify Perception- Ozium, tobacco dust, and air rune dust<br>
Fortify Intelligence- Sweet leaf dust, moondust, and air rune dust<br>
Fortify Constitution- Bone, any raw fish, and earth rune dust<br>
Fortify Endurance- Leeches, an apple, and earth rune dust<br>
Poison- Any two: dirt, poison berries, raw sweetleaf, or raw pipeweed<br>
Deadly Poison- Add gold dust during poison brewing.<br>

<br><br>
<h4>Enchanting:</h4><br>
Most steel and gilded weaponry can be enchanted, as well as bows.<br>
Enchanting is done on an etcher using a hammer, then finished on a regular forge.<br>
A rune must be placed on a nearby plinth, and you'll need gold dust.<br>
Magic Dust- 1 dust each of: Fire rune, water rune, air rune, and earth rune.<br>
Etcher- 2 iron bars, 1 small log, 1 magic dust.<br>
Plinth- 2 small logs.
